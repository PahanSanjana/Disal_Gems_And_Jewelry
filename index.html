About Us

Who We Are

Disal Ceylon Gems & Jewelry is a distinguished luxury brand hailing from Sri Lanka, renowned for its exceptional craftsmanship and authentic gemstone collections. With deep roots in the island's rich heritage, we specialize in creating exquisite jewelry that marries traditional artistry with contemporary elegance.

What We Do

Our commitment extends beyond creating beautiful jewelry. We ethically source the finest gemstones, ensuring each piece meets the highest standards of quality and authenticity. Every creation is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans, and we proudly offer global shipping to bring Sri Lankan excellence to discerning customers worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to celebrate and share the unparalleled craftsmanship of Sri Lanka with the world. We strive to preserve traditional techniques while embracing modern design sensibilities, creating timeless pieces that tell stories of heritage, passion, and luxury.

Why Choose Us

Authentic Sri Lankan Gems

Directly sourced from the finest mines in Sri Lanka, each gemstone is certified and carries the authenticity that only our heritage can provide.

Expert Craftsmanship

Master artisans with decades of experience handcraft each piece, ensuring exceptional quality and attention to detail in every creation.

Ethical & Sustainable

We prioritize ethical sourcing and sustainable practices, ensuring that every gemstone is obtained responsibly with respect for people and the environment.

Global Reach

We deliver our luxury jewelry worldwide, bringing the beauty of Sri Lankan craftsmanship to international clients with secure, insured shipping.
